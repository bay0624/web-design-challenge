Max Temperature


A plot showing the maximum temperature vs the latitude of 500+ cities. As one would expect, the temparture becomes warmer the closer you get to the equator.

In the Northern hemisphere there is a strong negative correlation between Temperature and Latitude. The higher the latitude, the lower the temperature. In the southern hemisphere, we see there's a strong correlation between the temperature and the latitude. The lower the latitude, the lower the temperature.
